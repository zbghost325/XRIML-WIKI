# Testing Room

![Testing Room](../images/testing-room.jpg)

The Testing Room is our largest space, specifically designed for multi-user room-scale XR experiences.

## Room Specifications

| Spec | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Size** | 857.08 sq ft |
| **Capacity** | 20 people |
| **Ceiling Height** | 11 ft (with grid system for mounting tracking equipment) |
| **Workstations** | 9 PC workstations |

## Workstation Setup

Each of the 9 workstations includes:

- **Play Area:** 6.5 ft × 6.5 ft outlined on carpet with safety bollard barriers
- **Headset:** Meta Quest 3 (wireless and wired capable)
- **Stylus:** Logitech MX Ink
- **Tracking:** Inside-out tracking

## Room Configurations

The Testing Room supports flexible configurations:

| Configuration | Details |
|---------------|---------|
| **Room-scale with PCs** | 9 stations (6.5 ft × 6.5 ft each) |
| **Room-scale without PCs** | 2 additional spaces |
| **Stationary (sit/stand)** | Up to 16 stations (3 ft × 3 ft each) |

## Additional Equipment

- 2 wall-mounted TVs with A/V input
- Grid ceiling system for mounting tracking and other equipment

## Best Used For

- Multi-user XR experiences and demonstrations
- Class sessions requiring multiple simultaneous users
- Large-scale testing and user studies
- VR presentations and showcases
