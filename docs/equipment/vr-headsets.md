!!! tip "Which headset should I use?"
    For most coursework and projects, the **Meta Quest 3** is recommended — it's standalone, portable, and supports both VR and MR. For high-fidelity PC rendering, use the **HTC Vive Pro 2** or **Valve Index** at a lab workstation.
