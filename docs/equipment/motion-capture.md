Body and hand tracking motion capture systems available in the XRIML.

## Available Systems

| System | Type | Location | Notes |
|--------|------|----------|-------|
| Rokoko Smartsuit Pro II | Inertial (full body) | Lab use only | 19 sensors, real-time streaming to Unity/Unreal |
| Rokoko Smartgloves | Inertial (hand) | Lab use only | Finger capture, pairs with Smartsuit |
| OptiTrack | Optical (marker-based) | Testing Room | High-precision, multi-camera setup |

!!! warning "Training Required"
    Motion capture equipment requires an orientation session before use. Contact the lab to schedule training.
